### 🧠 What drives me

I'm a B.Tech Data Science & Engineering student at **IIT Mandi**, and honestly — I just love figuring out *how things work* and then making them work better. Give me a messy problem and a blank repo, and I'm happy.

My world right now revolves around:

- 🤖 **AI / ML & GenAI** — fine-tuning models, RAG pipelines, self-supervised learning, anything that learns from data
- 👁️ **Computer Vision & NLP** — transformers, vision models, the occasional Grad-CAM rabbit hole
- ⛓️ **Web3 & Blockchain** — smart contracts, dApps, on-chain logic that actually solves real-world problems
- 🧩 **Competitive Programming** — Codeforces Specialist, LeetCode Knight, and a genuine addiction to solving hard problems
- 🌐 **Full-Stack Development** — React, Node, FastAPI — the glue that turns ideas into usable products
